About:

Welcome to SockX! We strive to be a great asset for those selling or buying shoes. SockX is not just a parody name, its an acronym. Selling Only Cool Kicks is what we're all about! Oh yeah, and the X just stands for exchange. Anyways, feel free to browse around posts created by other users and find your feet their new best friend.

Buying:

When browsing our site please keep in mind you may be able to snag a deal by checking if you can bid on shoes. Or if you're not feeling too competitive you can just buy them now at the prices listed.

Selling:

Do you have shoes? Do you want money? Feel free to upload your own post selling shoes you no longer wear or want! Keep in mind all fields when adding a shoe to our site requires an input for them all or you cannot post and be sure to add a photo of the shoe you're selling!

Reviews:

Reviews that show up on your post do not directly relate to the shoe being sold. Reviews on our site are for people who own/owned the same pair of shoes so customers can get further info on the pair they may be browsing.
